# 🎮 Cliky - Clicker Game

A fun and addictive clicker game where you earn coins by clicking and investing in upgrades!

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Click the big button** to earn coins
2. **Buy upgrades** with your coins to earn passive income
3. **Watch your wealth grow** as upgrades generate coins automatically
4. Each upgrade increases in cost, but generates more coins per second

## 🚀 Features

- **5 Upgrades**: Cursor, Farmer, Factory, Robot, and AI
- **Progressive Cost Scaling**: Each upgrade becomes more expensive as you buy more
- **Passive Income**: Upgrades generate coins automatically
- **Auto-Save**: Game progress is automatically saved to your browser
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Floating Text Animation**: Visual feedback for clicks and income

## 🎮 Play Online

Open `index.html` in your web browser to play!

## 💾 Saving

Your progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Your coins and upgrades will be preserved even if you close the browser!

## 🔄 Reset

Click the "Reset Game" button at the bottom to start over. This will clear all progress from your browser.

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- **Frontend Only**: No backend required, runs entirely in the browser
- **Local Storage**: Uses browser's localStorage for saving progress
- **Vanilla JavaScript**: No dependencies or frameworks needed
- **Responsive CSS**: Mobile-friendly design
